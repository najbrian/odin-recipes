Lasagna

Description

This is lasagna. You take uncooked pasta sheets, add marinara and cheese, and layer above.

Cook at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Basil
  • Any additional condiments

Steps to cook

  1. Layer a pasta sheet
  2. add marinara
  3. add cheese
  4. continue to your desired amount
  5. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  6. cook for 45-90 minutes
